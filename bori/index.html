Our Story

Born in the Heart of
Central Asia

BORI was created with a simple mission: deliver world-class apparel without the world-class markup. From our 5,000+ sqm facility in Kazakhstan's Special Economic Zone, we design, cut, sew, and ship gear that rivals the best — at prices that make sense.

Our name means "wolf" in Kazakh — an animal that embodies strength, endurance, and intelligence. Every BORI garment carries that spirit.
