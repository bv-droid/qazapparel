01

Brand Overview

Qazaqstan Apparel is a premium contract manufacturing company based in Astana, Kazakhstan. The brand identity reflects precision, quality, and the ambition of Central Asian manufacturing on the global stage.

Mission

To deliver world-class apparel manufacturing from Kazakhstan, combining international quality standards with competitive pricing and strategic location advantages.

Vision

To become Central Asia's leading apparel manufacturer and the preferred production partner for global brands in tactical, workwear, outdoor, and sport categories.

Values

Quality without compromise. Honest pricing. Full transparency. Innovation in manufacturing. Pride in origin.

02

Logo

The Qazaqstan Apparel logo consists of a stylized "Q" mark (symbol) and a wordmark. The "Q" is formed by a bold navy ring with a gold arrow tail, symbolizing forward momentum and growth.

Logo Variations

Primary — Light Background
Primary — Dark Background
Symbol Only
Monochrome — Brand Color BG

Clear Space & Minimum Size

Clear Space

Minimum clear space around the logo equals the height of the "Q" tail arrow. No text, graphics, or other logos should intrude into this zone.

Minimum Size

Full logo: min 120px wide (digital), 30mm (print).
Symbol only: min 24px (digital), 8mm (print).

Logo Usage Rules

Do

  • Use approved color variations only
  • Maintain proportions when scaling
  • Use on solid backgrounds with sufficient contrast
  • Use the animated version for digital hero sections
  • Pair with Inter font for wordmark

Don't

  • Stretch, rotate, or distort the logo
  • Change logo colors outside approved palette
  • Place on busy or low-contrast backgrounds
  • Add shadows, gradients, or effects to the logo
  • Rearrange the Q symbol and wordmark

05

Brand Voice & Tone

Qazaqstan Apparel speaks with authority and warmth. Professional but approachable. Confident but never arrogant.

Professional

We communicate with precision and expertise. Technical competence shows through clear, specific language about manufacturing processes and quality standards.

Confident

We know our capabilities and express them directly. No hedging. We benchmark against the best and deliver. "World-class patterns. Mid-range pricing."

Ambitious

We look forward and outward. Made in Kazakhstan is not a limitation — it's an origin story. Central Asia is our launchpad, not our ceiling.

Language Guidelines

Say

  • "Premium quality at honest prices"
  • "Built for scale. Designed for quality."
  • "Made in Kazakhstan" (with pride)
  • "From concept to delivery"
  • "Your manufacturing partner"

Don't say

  • "Cheap" or "budget" (use "competitive")
  • "Copy" or "replica" (use "benchmarked")
  • "Third world" manufacturing
  • Overly technical jargon without context
  • Vague promises without specifics

07

Applications

How the brand system applies across different touchpoints.

Website

Dark hero with navy/gold palette. Light body sections alternate with navy-dark feature sections. Gold for CTAs and accents. Grid pattern on dark backgrounds.

Social Media

Navy-deep backgrounds for impact posts. Gold accents for CTAs and highlights. Sub-brand posts use direction-specific colors (tactical green, workwear amber, outdoor green, sport blue).

Print & Packaging

Business cards: navy stock, gold foil logo. Packaging: kraft with navy label. Documents: white with navy header bar and gold rule lines.
